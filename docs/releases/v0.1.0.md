# Kybernetes v0.1.0

Date: 2026-07-17

Kybernetes v0.1.0 makes the governor canonical and runtime harnesses adaptive.
Users provide outcomes, constraints, timing, and risk boundaries; Kybernetes
selects and governs the smallest reliable execution substrate.

## Highlights

- Five pressure-tested `kybernetes:*` skills with one canonical governor.
- Durable lifecycle, manifest, capability, workgraph, activation, recovery,
  verification, learning, and retirement contracts.
- Codex, Claude Code, and portable bindings that inspect actual capabilities.
- 68 pressure scenarios and deterministic CI validators.

## Upgrade

Reinstall or update from the repository, list available skills, and select the
skills supported by your agent. Existing portable `control.md` and
`verification.md` records remain valid; add `manifest.md` when cross-turn module
reconstruction is needed.

## Limits

Runtime-native operations remain advisory and capability-dependent. The v0.1
portable workgraph uses revision checks, idempotency, cooperative leases, and
single-writer reconciliation rather than a transactional backend.

Verification details: [v0.1.0-verification.md](v0.1.0-verification.md).
