# Code Of Conduct

Kybernetes follows a simple standard: be respectful, concrete, and oriented toward making the work better.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat contributors with respect.
- Critique ideas and changes, not people.
- Explain disagreements with evidence or examples.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this standard.
